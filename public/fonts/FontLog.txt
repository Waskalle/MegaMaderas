FONTLOG for the Germano font
==========================
This file provides detailed information on the Germano font.
This information should be distributed along with the Germano font and any
derivative works.

Basic Font Information
----------------------
Germano is based on Open Sans Condensed by Steve Matheson, with adding of a
BoldOblique Typeface, and changing to OpenType CFF. Germano is released under the Apache
